# AI Digital Twin

Create your digital avatar from a photo.

## Features

- Upload your photo
- Create AI-powered digital twin
- Download your avatar

## Usage

1. Go to the website
2. Upload your photo
3. Click "Create My Digital Twin"
4. Download your avatar
